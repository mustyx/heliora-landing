Our Services

01 — Solar Mini-Grid Engineering Design

Isolated & interconnected
mini-grids. Engineered right.

Heliora's core expertise is the full engineering design of solar mini-grids for rural electrification — both isolated off-grid systems and grid-interconnected configurations. We deliver bankable, NERC-compliant designs from concept through construction-ready drawings.

Isolated Mini-Grid Design
Off-grid solar-battery systems engineered for unserved rural communities, 50kW–1MW
Grid-Interconnected Design
Hybrid mini-grids designed for eventual NESI grid integration with protection coordination
System Sizing & Modelling
HOMER Pro & PVSyst simulation, load forecasting, battery storage optimisation
Detailed Engineering Package
Single-line diagrams, protection relay settings, civil drawings, bills of materials

02 — Owner's Engineer Services

Your independent eyes
on every project.

Heliora acts as the independent Owner's Engineer — protecting your interests during construction, commissioning, and operation. We verify that your EPC contractor delivers what was designed, on spec, on time, and to the agreed performance guarantees.

Construction Supervision Technical Due Diligence Commissioning

03 — Feasibility Studies & Energy Audits

Know before
you commit capital.

Comprehensive technical and financial feasibility assessments — site evaluation, solar resource analysis, load profiling, system sizing, financial modelling (NPV, IRR, payback), and investment memoranda. For mini-grids (50kW–1MW) and C&I solar.

Site Assessment Financial Modelling Load Profiling

04 — Environmental & Social Impact Assessment

IFC-compliant.
Lender-ready.

Full ESIAs compliant with Nigeria's EIA Act and IFC Performance Standards. Environmental & Social Management Plans, stakeholder engagement, gender inclusion assessments, and FMEnv permit support. Built to satisfy DFI and lender requirements.

IFC Performance Standards FMEnv Permits

05 — C&I Solar Project Design & Development

Large-scale solar for
factories, telecoms & estates.

Rooftop and ground-mount C&I solar (200kW–2MW) — PVSyst performance modelling, grid integration studies, EPC procurement support, construction supervision, and performance acceptance testing.

PVSyst Modelling Grid Integration

06 — Solar Home System Design

Technical design for
residential & PAYG solar.

Component selection, electrical wiring diagrams, bills of materials, installation guidelines, and QA specifications for solar home systems from 10W to 500W. Serving SHS distributors and PAYG solar operators.

10W – 500W Systems PAYG Design
